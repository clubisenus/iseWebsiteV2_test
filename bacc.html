About the competition

Faced with real world industry challenges, participants will have to find optimal solutions that stands out from the crowd.
Think you've got what it takes? Pit yourself against the best minds in Singapore, and show us what you got!
Every participant will receive an exclusive goodie bag, including a 64GB USB drive.
The top 3 teams of each category for each round will walk away with cash prizes.

For the first time ever, the competition will be hosted online, so join us from the comforts of your home!

Eligibility

Pre-University Category:
Open to students currently enrolled in junior colleges, polytechnics or upper secondary in Singapore

University Category:
Open to all NUS ISEM undergraduates

Sign up in teams of four!

prizes

For both the Pre-University and Undergraduate categories, there will be 2 rounds of competition; the qualitative segment on the first week, followed by quantitative on the second.

The top 3 teams of each rounds, for each respective categories, will each take home a prize.

Yes, you read right. There 4 sets of Top 3 prizes up for grabs!

Finally, all participants will receive the following after the event, but only if their submissions for both rounds has been made successfully before the deadline.

  • 64GB Micron USB 3.0 drive
  • Certificate of Accomplishment
  • Goodie Bag

FAQ

Given the current situation with COVID-19, how will the competition be held?
As mentioned, the competition this year will be held online, via video conference and online submissions. More details will be emailed to you!

How will I receive my 64GB Micron USB 3.0 Drive?
Post competition, the USB drive, along with the certificates and goodie bags will be sent to your schools.

Must I sign up in a team of 4?
Yes.

Must I know how to code?
No, participants will be taught the required knowledge during the competition!

Where can I find event updates?
This website will be updated regularly, should there be updates. Registered teams will also receive emails from the organising committee should there be any event updates. Alternatively, you may follow us on Instagram, @nus_ise! If you have any further enquiries, do reach out to us at bacc2020@nusiseclub.com!

What time will the event be?
Over the 2 Saturdays, the event will start at 9am each day, and end in the afternoon at about 4pm. More details will be released closer to the dates!
